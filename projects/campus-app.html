Divide&Conquer

Data-backed strategy work that broke complex workflows into clear ownership, sequencing, and execution paths.

Role: Product Strategy / Operations Timeline: 2025 Tools: Workflow Mapping, Prioritization, Analytics

Problem

Complex workstreams can become overwhelming when responsibilities, priorities, and dependencies are not clearly separated.

Goal

Create a structured product strategy approach that breaks large ambiguous workflows into clear, actionable launch paths.

Research / Process

Audited the current workflow, grouped recurring blockers, and identified the highest-value areas for sequencing and ownership.

Divide&Conquer Process Map

Solution

Built a divide-and-prioritize framework that clarified decision points, reduced ambiguity, and helped the team move from scattered tasks to focused execution.

Divide&Conquer Strategy Preview

Final Outcome

Delivered a cleaner roadmap structure with clearer responsibilities, sequencing, and measurable follow-through.

Impact

Improved visibility into what mattered most and made the next steps easier for collaborators to act on.

Reflection

Good product work often starts by making the messy middle legible enough for people to move with confidence.
