UX/UI Design · 2025

UH Energy Website

A unified web experience turning dense UH Energy research into clear pathways for students, professionals, and partners.

Role: UX/UI Designer & Researcher Timeline: March 2025 - May 2025 Tools: Content Architecture, UX Research, Design Systems

Problem

Course discovery and conversion suffered due to inconsistent page structure, dense research content, and navigation friction.

Goal

Improve certification-page conversion and strengthen navigation clarity for prospective learners.

Research / Process

Redesigned 21+ certification pages, validated information architecture, and aligned page hierarchy to scanning behavior.

UH Energy Process Snapshot

Solution

Built a unified web pattern that turns dense energy research into scannable pathways for students and professionals.

UH Energy Solution Preview

Final Outcome

Shipped an updated page system and backlog strategy for future optimization.

Impact

Increased conversion across 3,000+ users and created a stronger foundation for future site improvements.

Reflection

Combining product content strategy with implementation review helped us move quickly without sacrificing quality.
