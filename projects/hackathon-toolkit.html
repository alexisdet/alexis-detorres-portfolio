DEMI

Role: Product Manager / UX Designer Timeline: 48-hour hackathon Tools: AI Workflows, UX Research, Rapid Prototyping

Problem

Students needed a clearer way to plan academic paths without bouncing between disconnected degree requirements, schedules, and advising notes.

Goal

Build an AI-assisted planning platform that turns confusing academic decisions into a more guided, confidence-building workflow.

Research / Process

Mapped student planning pain points, prioritized the highest-friction moments, and converted those insights into a fast product flow.

DEMI Research & Planning

Solution

Designed DEMI as a practical academic planning assistant with clearer recommendations, structured next steps, and lightweight decision support.

DEMI Prototype Direction

Final Outcome

Delivered a polished hackathon prototype and product story ready for judging and stakeholder presentation.

Impact

Placed 2nd for the academic planning platform concept by combining product clarity, AI workflow thinking, and fast execution.

Reflection

The strongest idea was not adding more information, but organizing decisions so students could move forward faster.
